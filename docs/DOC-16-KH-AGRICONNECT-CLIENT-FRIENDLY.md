# KH Agriconnect - Client-Friendly Overview

## 1. Project Overview

### What is KH Agriconnect?
- KH Agriconnect is a digital platform that connects farmers with tractor operators and service providers.
- It makes it easier for farmers to find, book, and pay for agricultural services in one place.

### What problem does it solve?
- Removes the need for farmers to search for operators manually.
- Reduces delays and uncertainty in booking tractor services.
- Helps operators get more work and get paid faster.
- Gives the platform owner control and visibility over the entire process.

## 2. Key Features

### Farmer features
- Easy login with mobile verification.
- Search available tractors and services.
- Book a service quickly.
- Pay a part of the amount upfront and the rest after completion.
- Track the job status and view progress.
- Submit complaints or reviews if needed.

### Operator features
- Simple registration process.
- Add tractor details and documents.
- Get approval from the admin before going live.
- Receive booking requests and accept jobs.
- Update service progress and complete jobs.
- View earnings and job history.

### Admin features
- Approve or reject new operators and tractors.
- Manage service pricing and offers.
- Monitor bookings, payments, and system activity.
- Handle refund requests and customer support issues.
- Broadcast important messages or announcements.

## 3. User Roles

### Farmer
- The farmer is the customer who books tractor services.
- They can request service, pay, and track progress.

### Operator
- The operator is the service provider with tractors.
- They register, add their tractor, and accept jobs.

### Admin
- The admin manages the platform and approves operators.
- They ensure the system runs smoothly and safely.

### Super Admin
- The super admin has the highest access.
- They can create and manage admin users.
- They oversee overall platform safety and settings.

## 4. How the System Works (Flow)

### Farmer flow
- Login with phone verification.
- Search available tractors or services.
- Book the service and confirm the job.
- Pay an advance amount.
- Track service arrival and progress.
- Pay the remaining amount when the work is done.

### Operator flow
- Register on the platform.
- Add tractor details and required documents.
- Wait for admin approval.
- Receive booking requests.
- Accept bookings and complete the job.

### Admin flow
- Review operator and tractor applications.
- Approve or reject registrations.
- Monitor active bookings and payments.
- Resolve issues and manage platform policies.

## 5. Payment Flow (Simple)

### Advance payment
- Farmer pays a partial amount when booking.
- This secures the service and confirms the operator.

### Remaining payment
- The rest of the payment is made after the service is complete.
- This ensures operators deliver the job before full payment.

### Refund
- If something goes wrong, the farmer can request a refund.
- The admin reviews the refund request and decides.
- The system tracks refund status clearly.

## 6. Notifications

- Users receive updates when their booking changes.
- Farmers get alerts about booking confirmation, operator arrival, and payment status.
- Operators get alerts for new booking requests and updates.
- Admins get notifications for approval tasks and important events.

## 7. Admin Control & Safety

### Verification system
- Operators and tractors must be verified before they can work.
- Admins check documents and approve trusted operators.

### Fraud prevention
- Payments are handled carefully to reduce risk.
- Booking and refund actions are reviewed by admins.
- The system keeps records of important actions.

## 8. Business Benefits

### For farmers
- Faster access to tractor services.
- Cleaner booking and payment process.
- Better visibility into job status.

### For operators
- Easier access to more customers.
- Clear payment and job tracking.
- Better chances to grow business.

### For platform owner
- A managed marketplace for farm services.
- Control over approvals, pricing, and safety.
- Revenue potential from service bookings and commissions.
